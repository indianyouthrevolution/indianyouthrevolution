About I.Y.R

Vision • Mission • Values

Vision

An India where every young person practices responsibility, empathy and courage to create positive change.

Mission

Inspire and empower youth through motivation, education support, skill-building, community development, daily Deen & humanity values, and research on human behaviour.

Values

Integrity • Service • Knowledge • Unity • Consistency.

📜 Our Journey

📥 Download Profile (PDF)
2017
💡 Idea Spark

Yusuf Khan envisioned a youth-led movement rooted in responsibility, unity, and compassion — the seed of IYR was planted.

2020
🤝 Community Actions

During tough times, IYR initiated charity drives, academic support, and awareness campaigns — proving youth can lead with empathy.

2024
📖 Daily Deen & Values

Started daily reflections on Hadith, Sunnah, and moral values — connecting faith with humanity and everyday action.

2025
🚀 IYR Programs Expand

Launched full-scale youth empowerment: scholarships, skill workshops, clean-up drives, and leadership training — IYR became a movement.

Key Programs

What we run across communities

Motivation & Mentoring

Talks, peer circles, and consistent guidance to build discipline and purpose.

Education & Scholarships

Resources and support for students who need financial and academic help.

Community Development

Clean‑up drives, health awareness, and neighbourhood initiatives with youth leadership.

Skill‑Building

Design, video editing, coding basics, and career readiness workshops.

Deen & Humanity Messages

Daily reminders on Hadith, Sunnah, and universal ethics for a better self.

Research on Human Nature

Projects exploring behaviour and brain basics to improve learning & growth.
